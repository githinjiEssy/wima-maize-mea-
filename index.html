Some common questions
were often asked

How do you ensure the quality of your maize flour?

Quality is our top priority. We carefully select high-quality maize grains and subject them to rigorous quality control measures throughout the processing stages. Our state-of-the-art facilities and strict adherence to quality standards ensure that our maize flour consistently meets the highest quality benchmarks.

Where do you source your maize grains from?

We source our maize grains from local farmers who share our commitment to quality and sustainability. We prioritize building strong relationships with farmers, ensuring fair prices and supporting local agricultural communities.

Do you offer bulk purchasing options for businesses?

Yes, we offer bulk purchasing options for businesses, distributors, and retailers. Please contact our sales team for more information on bulk orders and wholesale pricing.

How can I store maize flour to maintain its freshness?

To maintain the freshness of maize flour, store it in a cool, dry place in an airtight container. Avoid exposure to moisture, heat, and direct sunlight, as these can affect the quality and shelf life of the flour.

Are your maize flour products enriched with nutrients

Yes, our maize flour products are packed with essential nutrients, vitamins, and minerals. We understand the importance of offering wholesome and nutritious options for our customers' well-being.

Are your maize grains sourced sustainably?

Yes, we are committed to sustainable sourcing practices. We work closely with local farmers who follow environmentally friendly farming methods and support sustainable agriculture.
